About

I'm Niklas Moll, a Fashion Management student at Texoversum Nagold with a background spanning premium automotive retail, Mercedes-Benz marketing, and hands-on experience at Chanel GmbH in Munich.

My work lives at the intersection of brand storytelling, visual identity, and strategic thinking. I believe the best fashion experiences are built on a sophisticated blend of timeless elegance and forward-thinking innovation.

Beyond academics, I founded the X Run Club in Nagold — a community of 25+ members — and am an active member of Duchesse Nagold e.V., staying connected to the pulse of the fashion and textiles industry.
